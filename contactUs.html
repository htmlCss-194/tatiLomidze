EMERGECENCY SITUATIONS

After hours emergencies include urgent situations regarding the safety and welfare of U.S. citizens may include:

  • Violent crime victims
  • Arrests
  • Deaths
  • Child abductions
  • Life-threatening medical emergencies (but call 112 (24/7) first to request urgent medical assistance from Georgian government authorities)
  • Other serious safety and welfare issues of a U.S. citizen
